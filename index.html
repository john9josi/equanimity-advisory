Why

Software is cheap now. Building is fast. Shipping is faster. The gap between idea and product has nearly collapsed. But that’s true for everyone. Your competitors are shipping just as fast as you are.

Your edge is no longer speed. It’s strategy.

The founders who win aren’t the ones who ship the most. They’re the ones who know their customers so deeply — the data, the conversations, the patterns no one else is paying attention to — that every decision, move, or dollar compounds into an edge your competitors can’t copy.

That kind of knowledge doesn’t come from moving faster. It comes from its opposite. From stepping back, finding your center, and looking at your market and customers with clarity.

That’s equanimity: the calm, grounded presence that lets you see what’s actually there, not what the noise is telling you to chase.

That’s what we help fintech founders do. Know deeply. Build boldly. Launch decisively.

About

Equanimity Advisory was founded by John Josi, a product strategist and advisor who has taken fintech products to market across bill payment, invoice financing, credit cards, and checking accounts. John has worked at, invested in, and advised companies from seed through Series D, including Ramp, SoFi, and Adyen.

We started Equanimity Advisory because early-stage founders deserve the same quality of customer and market intelligence that well-resourced teams take for granted — without the overhead of a full-time hire.

When not working with founders, John hosts a radio show, writes poetry, and volunteers in his home community in West Marin.
