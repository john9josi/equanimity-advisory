Services

This is how we work.

The engagement is one continuous relationship, structured in two phases.

Phase 1 — Getting Clear Month 1

We go deep on your customers, your competitive landscape, and your positioning. We sit in your data, talk to your users, map the market, and synthesize it into something you can act on. You walk away with a segmentation, a competitive SWOT, and a positioning framework — worked through together in a live session.

Phase 2 — Staying Sharp Ongoing

Ongoing advisory as you build. Product decisions, GTM moves, fundraise prep, distribution strategy. Calls, async, and occasionally in person. The clarity we built together becomes the lens for every decision that follows.

About

John Josi is an advisor and product strategist with six years of fintech experience across Ramp, SoFi, and Adyen — spanning strategy, product, go-to-market, and Chief of Staff roles. He has created customer segmentation and lead scoring models, taken bill payment platforms and invoice financing products to market, and turned around large-scale business lines and launched credit cards from 0→1. Previously, he scaled a tequila brand to strategic exit, touching everything from customer service to quality assurance.

He started Equanimity Advisory to help early-stage fintech founders do the thing that most skip: get genuinely rigorous about their customers and their market before their next big move.

He lives in West Marin, where he hosts a local radio show, writes poetry, runs a civic-focused AI studio, and involves himself in community happenings.
